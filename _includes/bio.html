Bio

I am a third-year Computer Science PhD student in SysNet area at UIUC working with Prof. Tianyin Xu.

I am generally interested in Systems, in particular, Distributed System and Operating Systems. My current research focuses on improving the reliability of cloud system management in the context of Kubernetes.

I received my B.S. Computer Science from University of Wisconsin-Madison. I was an undergrad Research Assitant in Paradyn Tools Project group at UW-Madison, advised by Prof. Barton Miller. I also worked with Prof. Earlence Fernandes on an adversarial ML project.
